Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device and encrypted with AES-256-GCM. We never upload your data to any server.

How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Backup & Restore ‚Üí Create Backup. You can save the backup file to Files, iCloud Drive, or share it.

Can I use the app for my whole family?

Yes! You can create profiles for each family member and track their records separately.

What happens if I delete the app?

All locally stored data will be deleted. Make sure to create a backup before uninstalling if you want to keep your records.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Language and select your preferred language (English or Vietnamese).

Is FamilyLog for professional use?

No. FamilyLog is for personal record-keeping only. It is not intended for professional or commercial use.
