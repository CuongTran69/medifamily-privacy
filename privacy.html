Introduction

FamilyLog ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Family Records: Event information, notes, details, contact names
  • Family Member Profiles: Names, dates of birth, relationships, personal details
  • Photos: Documents and records you choose to capture
  • Reminders: Appointment schedules and event reminders

Automatically Collected Data

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Store and organize your family's records
  • Send reminder notifications for appointments and events
  • Provide backup and restore functionality
